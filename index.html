What’s inside

  • 02Visual Modelwatches the camera feed and describes what it sees
  • 03Telegram Botyour remote, simple enough to use from any phone
  • 04Raspberry Piruns the on board logic locally for lower latency
  • 05Your Droneany flight stack that can receive motion commands
